Aside from Business ...

Growing up, I've been fortunate that my family and I have been able to do a lot of travelling. I've been to over 30 Cities in the US alone, as well as 7 different Countries around the world, and spend around 4 months of the year in Naples, Florida.
I've grown a love for meeting new people, seeing new things, and embracing different cultures. A highlight of all my travel experiences has been going back to where my Father grew up in Italy. It was a moment where I was able to appreciate everything we have and take for granted for in Canada, while at the same time appreciating the beauty of his small town.
